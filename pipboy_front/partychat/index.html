PARTYCHAT

Welcome to PartyChat, the last group chat!

Use ./partychat command to fire up the client.

Instructions:

  • Use !list command to see who's online.
  • Then just !say something! Everybody mentioned in your message will see it.
  • You can also view !history of a conversation: quite useful if somebody said something while you were offline and couldn't see it.

Example:

(my name is @krait)
> !list
- @alice
- @bob
- @krait
> !say hey, @alice ! and you, @bob
: @krait said: hey, @alice ! and you, @bob
: @alice said: sup
: @bob said: 
: @bob said: damn partychat ate my message again, i hope the guy who made it burns in hell

Enjoy!
